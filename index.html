SIMElab Africa

Social Media Lab Africa

SIMElab is an interdisciplinary center for Research in Big Data and Social Media Analytics housed at USIU-Africa's Freida Brown Innovation Center.

SIMElab Africa offers a research and development environment to USIU-Africa faculty and students, civil society and coporate businesses, and policymakers in Kenya and beyond

SIMElab is jointly funced by the USA Embassy in Nairobi and USIU-Africa
